User Guide

In order to browse through our vast range of products, Please register as a user or Login in if you are already a member by clicking on the Account icon in the right of navigation bar. Upon creating your account, you may browse the products of your choice by selecting appropriate category of products from the down down menu of "Products Categories" on the navigation panel or by searching for a particular product using the search bar in the upper right corner. After finding the product(s) of your choice, you may add it to your cart by clicking the button "Add to Cart" next to the item of your choice. Once satisfied with your selections, click on the shopping cart. Once you Verify your choice of products, you can either remove product from the cart by clicking the remove button next to each item on the table or even continue shopping going back to the index/products page. Once finalised, click on checkout and confirm your order to have it delivered to you shortly.
